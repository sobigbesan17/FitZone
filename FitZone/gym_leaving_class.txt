Leaving Class Information:
---
Please note the following important information about leaving or cancelling a gym class:

• Cancellation Policy: You may cancel your class booking up to 2 hours before the class start time without penalty.
• Late Cancellations: Cancellations made within 2 hours of the class start time may count as a no-show.
• No-Show Policy: Repeated no-shows may result in restrictions on future class bookings.
• Waitlist: If you cancel your booking, your spot may be given to a member on the waitlist.
• Refunds: Class cancellations do not qualify for refunds as they are included in your membership.

If you have any questions about our cancellation policy, please speak to a member of our team at reception or contact us at classes@fitzone.co.uk
